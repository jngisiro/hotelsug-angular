Arcadia Suites - Kampala

54A, Kira Road, Kampala, Uganda

Arcadia Suites - Kampala offers accommodation in the heart of Kampala. It is also within walking distance of Aacia Mall and Kisementi retails village with all its vibrant culinary experiences. Free WiFi is offered throughout the property and free private parking is available on site.

All units feature 2 flat-screen TVs. Some units are air conditioned and include a seating area with a TV. There is also a kitchenette, equipped with a microwave. A refrigerator and kettle are also provided. Bed linen is available.

Arcadia Suites also includes a fitness centre. Guests can enjoy a meal at the on-site steakhouse rooftop restaurant, followed by a drink at the bar. The property also offers special diet menus.

Gaddafi National Mosque is 2.8 km from Arcadia Suites - Kampala. Independence Monument is 2.4 km from the property, while Mulago Hospital is 850 m away. The Uganda Golf Club is 2 km from Arcadia Suites.
